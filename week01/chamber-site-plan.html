Site Name: Setubal's Chamber of Commerce Plan

Why the Name?

The name was selected because Setubal is the nearest city to where I live.

Overview

Purpose

Setubal's Chamber of Commerce is responsible for improving the local business environment and strengthening communities through advocacy, networking, and campaigning. In addition, it creates an easy line of communication for business owners to connect with governing bodies. All of this will be facilitated through a stronger online presence.

Scenarios

  1. What networking activities are you aware of?

  2. How can you get more involved??

  3. What challenges are the local businesses facing?
