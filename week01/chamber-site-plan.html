Overview

Purpose

I am a student majoring in computer engineering, and I love basketball, particularly the NBA. Although I love staying up to date with what is happening in the NBA, my responsibilities make it difficult. Knowing that many people face similar dilemmas, I decided to build a website where busy NBA fans can have easy access to the most interesting and relevant information in the NBA, including news, game results, and standings.

Audience

My audience consists of college students who enjoy keeping up with the NBA. These people are remarkably busy with their studies and other responsibilities. These people want a reliable place where they can go whenever they have time, to find updates from all over the league, especially their teams. They will most likely be accessing the website through their laptops and smartphones. They will check the website during their study breaks, and they will spend most their time on the website at the end of the day when they are winding down.

Content

Home page

Where do you go when you only have a 10 minute break to find out what is going on in the NBA? How can you make sure you do not have to worry about FOMO? What was the score from last night's game? This website is designed to help NBA fans keep up with their favorite basketball league despite their busy schedules and different responsibilities. The goal of this website is to help all of its visitors quickly catch up with the NBA and find the most relevant information from the NBA when they manage to get a breather. This website will be your go to site to find out the latest and most relevant NBA transfers and rumors, scheduled games, final game scores, and more. Since you might not have 2 hours and 30 minutes to watch the full games as often as you would like, you will want to watch the 9 to 13 minute game highlights on our page. One of the things that separates our page from others is that while most pages are designed to make you spend a high amount of time on there, our page is designed to give you what you want quickly, without endlessly scrolling through the site. You do not have to provide any information in order to use the website.
